AUV APPLICATIONS

Information Collection

Unmanned environmental information collection - Temperature, pollution, mineral concentration from a remote underwater location at a low cost.

Marine Photography

Photography and videography of marine life for tourism and life monitoring, with less danger of environmental harm.

Underwater Mapping

Mapping of the ocean floor, or other water bodies such as rivers or lake. Finding lost objects or discovering the surface and aquatic life forms.

Consumer Product

Consumer product for marine researchers, hobbyists, with fully developed software and hardware, an underwater drone.

Wireless Underwater Communications

Underwater wireless communication between several vehicles for information sharing for a smarter and efficient data collection process.

Naval Border Monitoring

Collection of such vehicles can be used to constantly monitor the ocean borders and beaches for enhanced safety.

Structural Inspection

Underwater inspection and repair of structural or pipeline damages in extreme and hostile conditions.

Stealth Actions

Small form factor allows the vehicle to move undetected, can be used by the navy.
